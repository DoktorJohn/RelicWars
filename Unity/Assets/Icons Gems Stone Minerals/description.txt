Icons Gems Minerals Stone Metals

Richly detailed, hand-painted icons of gems, minerals, stones, and metals suitable for a strategy game, RPG, or boardgame-like experience.

- all icons are 128x128 pixels
- provided on a transparent background for maximum flexibility
- 83 icons (so far!)
- most gems come in rough, cut collection, and cut single versions
- stone comes in raw stones, cut block, ashlar (bricks), and gravel versions
- includes a few important ores
- inclues some metal ingots

Icons are provided as individual transparent-background PNG files. 

Feel free to email me and suggest more icons if you have any ideas!
